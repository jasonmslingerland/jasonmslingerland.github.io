How I get things done

Setting success criteria

Identifying the difference between success and failure helps keep one on task and focused.

Make a plan of attack

Minutes of planning can prevent hours of headache. Know what you are going to do before you do it.

Execute

Follow through the plan. If things didn't go as they expected, isolate the cause and fix it.

Fit and finish

Don't cut corners. Hold yourself to a high standard. If you aren't happy with your result, no one else will be.
